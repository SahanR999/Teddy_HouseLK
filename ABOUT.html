About Us

The Story of Teddy House!

Teddy House was founded in 2021 by Rammuthu Tharindu in Sri Lanka in response to the lack of teddy bear and chocolate shops that offered a premium experience at a fair price. Teddy House was born from this need, and since then, we have been dedicated to bringing you joy and comfort with our delightful range of teddies and chocolates. Our commitment is to provide top-notch quality and unforgettable experiences. If you are looking to buy premium teddies and chocolates at cheap prices, Teddy House is your go-to destination.

Teddy House Vision

Your Partner for Delightful Gifts

At Teddy House, our vision is to be the leading provider of premium teddies and chocolates in Sri Lanka, bringing joy and comfort to every home. We aim to create magical moments and lasting memories with every purchase, ensuring our products are more than just gifts—they are expressions of love and affection. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously innovate and expand our offerings, making Teddy House the go-to destination for delightful and heartwarming gifts.

Meet Our Founder

I founded Teddy House in 2021 because I saw a need for teddy bear and chocolate shops that offer a premium experience at a fair price. There simply wasn’t anything like that available for customers. Teddy House was born to fill that gap and bring joy to people’s lives. Our aim is to provide high-quality products that create memorable moments and bring comfort to every home.
